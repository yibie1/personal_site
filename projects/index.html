Sukie — Shop Management System

FlutterDartSQLiteOffline-First

Offline-first mobile shop management app for Ethiopian small business owners — inventory, sales, profits & credit book.

Sukie is a mobile app built for Ethiopian small business owners who want a simple, offline-first way to run their shop from their phone.

  • Inventory — add products with cost price, sell price, stock quantity, unit, and category. Automatic low-stock alerts.
  • Sales — record sales in seconds. Profit calculated automatically for every transaction.
  • Dashboard — today's revenue, profit, and items sold at a glance. Daily, weekly, and monthly charts.
  • Credit Book — track customers who take goods on credit, record partial payments, see outstanding balances.
  • Multi-currency — supports ETB, USD, EUR, and more.
  • Secure — license-key activation, username/password login, all data stored locally.

Works fully offline. No cloud account, no subscription — just install and start managing your shop.
